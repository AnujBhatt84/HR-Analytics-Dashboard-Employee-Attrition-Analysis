## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges faced by HR departments. Understanding why employees leave an organization is essential for improving employee retention and workforce planning.

This project analyzes HR data to identify patterns and insights related to employee attrition. An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was developed to visualize key HR metrics and provide actionable insights for decision-makers.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

HR departments often lack clear performance indicators to monitor workforce trends and employee attrition effectively. Without proper data visualization and analysis, it becomes difficult to:

* Track workforce size
* Monitor attrition levels
* Identify departments with higher turnover
* Understand employee demographics

This project aims to design and implement **HR KPIs and analytical dashboards** to help organizations make **data-driven HR decisions**.

---

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs were implemented to monitor workforce trends:

* 👥 **Employee Count** – Total number of employees in the organization
* 📉 **Attrition Count** – Number of employees who left the company
* 📊 **Attrition Rate** – Percentage of employees leaving the company
* 🧑‍💼 **Active Employees** – Current employees working in the organization
* 🎂 **Average Age** – Average age of employees in the workforce

These KPIs help HR teams measure workforce stability and employee turnover.

# 🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains HR employee information including:

* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Education Field
* Job Satisfaction

* Employee Demographics

This data was used to analyze workforce trends and identify attrition patterns.

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

Some important insights derived from the dashboard:

* Certain departments show significantly higher attrition rates.
* Younger employees tend to leave more frequently compared to senior employees.
* Attrition patterns vary across different education fields.
* Job satisfaction levels are closely related to employee retention.
